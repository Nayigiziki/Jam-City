Find Shows in your area

Upon login, fifty events in your area will be loaded. By clicking on an artist you can see where and when they are playing.

Discover New Music

Jamm-City makes it fun and exciting to explore new music. You can check out artists you haven't heard of that are touring in your city or search your favorite venues all over the world.

Create Playlists

Hit the 'add to spotify' button and three of the hottest tracks from that artist will be added to a playlist called Jamm-City. Which you can listen to anywhere that you have spotify.
